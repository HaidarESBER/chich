# Brand Identity: Nuage

## Status
Completed - Brand Identity Defined

## Brand Name Options

### Criteria Used
- Works in French and sounds premium
- Evokes quality/lifestyle, not "smoke shop"
- Short, memorable, domain-likely-available
- Avoids cliches (no "hookah heaven", "cloud nine", etc.)
- Works as a lifestyle brand, not just product category

---

### Option 1: **Nuage**

**Pronunciation:** noo-AZH
**Meaning/Association:** "Cloud" in French. Evokes the smoke/vapor experience in an elegant, abstract way. Poetic, soft, dreamlike. Associated with relaxation and floating sensations.

**Domain Status:**
- nuage.com - Likely taken (common word)
- nuage.fr - Likely taken
- nuagechicha.fr - More likely available
- shopnuage.fr - More likely available

**Tagline Suggestion:** "L'art de la detente" (The art of relaxation)

**Strengths:**
- One French word, immediately understandable
- Elegant, soft sound
- Works across lifestyle categories
- Evokes the experience without being literal

**Considerations:**
- Very common word, domain challenges
- May need modifier for uniqueness

---

### Option 2: **Braise**

**Pronunciation:** BREHZ
**Meaning/Association:** "Ember" in French. Evokes the glowing charcoal essential to hookah, warmth, and intimate gatherings. Suggests slow-burning quality and the ritual of preparation.

**Domain Status:**
- braise.com - Likely taken
- braise.fr - May be available
- maisonbraise.fr - More likely available
- labraise.fr - May be available

**Tagline Suggestion:** "Chaleur partagee" (Shared warmth)

**Strengths:**
- Strong, memorable sound
- Direct but sophisticated reference to hookah culture
- Evokes warmth, gathering, ritual
- Less common than "nuage"

**Considerations:**
- More niche association
- Could work well for the focused market

---

### Option 3: **Serenade**

**Pronunciation:** say-reh-NAHD
**Meaning/Association:** Musical evening performance, romantic gesture. Evokes evening relaxation, social moments, sophistication without direct product reference.

**Domain Status:**
- serenade.com - Likely taken
- serenade.fr - Likely taken
- serenadeparis.fr - More likely available

**Tagline Suggestion:** "Moments d'exception" (Exceptional moments)

**Strengths:**
- Premium, romantic connotation
- Works as pure lifestyle brand
- Memorable, musical quality
- No smoke/product reference - very abstract

**Considerations:**
- Less intuitive connection to product category
- May require stronger visual branding to communicate

---

### Option 4: **Salon Fume**

**Pronunciation:** sah-LON foo-MAY
**Meaning/Association:** "Smoke Room/Lounge" in French. References historical smoking rooms/lounges of the elite. Evokes sophistication, private clubs, refined leisure.

**Domain Status:**

- salonfume.fr - More likely available

**Tagline Suggestion:** "Le cercle des connaisseurs" (The connoisseurs' circle)

**Strengths:**

### Option 5: **Volute**

**Pronunciation:** voh-LOOT
**Meaning/Association:** Architectural/artistic term for spiral scroll shapes, also used to describe curling smoke. Combines aesthetic sophistication with smoke imagery elegantly.

**Domain Status:**
- volute.com - Likely taken

## Selected Name

### Nuage

**Pronunciation:** noo-AZH
**Meaning:** "Cloud" in French

**Tagline:** "L'art de la detente" (The art of relaxation)

**Domain Strategy:**
- Primary: nuagechicha.fr or shopnuage.fr
- Alternative: nuage-paris.fr

**Why Nuage:**
- Elegant, single French word with immediate understanding
- Evokes the smoke/vapor experience abstractly and poetically
- Soft, dreamlike quality aligns with relaxation positioning
- Works as a lifestyle brand beyond the product category
- Memorable and easy to pronounce in French and internationally

## Visual Direction

### Brand Personality
Nuage embodies the softness and ethereal quality of clouds. The visual identity should feel:
- **Serene** — Calm, unhurried, peaceful
- **Elevated** — Premium without pretension
- **Soft** — Gentle edges, no harsh contrasts
- **Modern** — Clean and contemporary
- **Inviting** — Warm and welcoming

### Mood Board Direction
The aesthetic draws from:
- Morning mist over calm water
- Soft natural light through sheer curtains
- Minimalist Scandinavian interiors
- High-end wellness and spa environments
- Contemporary Parisian apartments

## Typography

### Font Selection

| Role | Font | Weight | Fallback |
|------|------|--------|----------|
| **Headings** | Cormorant Garamond | 500, 600 | Georgia, serif |
| **Body** | Inter | 400, 500 | system-ui, sans-serif |

### Why These Fonts

**Cormorant Garamond (Headings):**
- Elegant serif with French typography heritage
- Light, airy letterforms echo the "cloud" concept
- Premium feel without being stuffy
- Excellent display characteristics
- Free on Google Fonts

**Inter (Body):**
- Clean, highly readable sans-serif
- Optimized for screens
- Complements the elegance of Cormorant
- Already established in project (from 01-01)
- Free on Google Fonts

### Typography Scale

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|---------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| H1 | Cormorant Garamond | 48px / 3rem | 500 | 1.2 |
| H2 | Cormorant Garamond | 36px / 2.25rem | 500 | 1.25 |
| H3 | Cormorant Garamond | 28px / 1.75rem | 500 | 1.3 |
| H4 | Inter | 20px / 1.25rem | 500 | 1.4 |
| Body | Inter | 16px / 1rem | 400 | 1.6 |
| Small | Inter | 14px / 0.875rem | 400 | 1.5 |
| Caption | Inter | 12px / 0.75rem | 400 | 1.4 |

### Typography Guidelines

1. **Heading usage:**
   - Use Cormorant Garamond for H1-H3 (brand moments)
   - Use Inter for H4 and below (functional headings)
   - Never bold Cormorant beyond 600 weight

2. **Body text:**
   - Default to Inter 400 for paragraphs
   - Use Inter 500 for emphasis (not bold)
   - Line length: 60-75 characters optimal

3. **Special treatments:**
   - Prices: Inter 500
   - Navigation: Inter 400, uppercase optional for main nav
   - Buttons: Inter 500, slight letter-spacing (0.02em)

## Style Guidelines

### Photography Direction

**Product Photography:**
- Soft, diffused natural lighting
- Neutral backgrounds (warm white, light stone, natural linen)
- Minimal props — let the product be the focus
- Subtle shadows, no harsh contrasts
- Lifestyle context when appropriate (hands, smoke wisps)

**Lifestyle Photography:**
- Aspirational but approachable
- Indoor settings: modern, minimalist interiors
- Warm, golden-hour lighting preferred
- Diverse representation
- Focus on moments of relaxation and connection
- Avoid cliched "hookah lounge" imagery

**Photo Editing:**
- Slightly desaturated, warm tones
- Soft contrast
- No heavy filters or vintage effects
- Consistent warmth across all images

### Spacing Philosophy

- **Generous whitespace** — Let content breathe
- **Editorial feel** — Like a premium magazine layout
- **Base unit:** 8px grid system
- **Section padding:** 64px-96px vertical
- **Card padding:** 24px-32px
- **Component spacing:** 16px-24px

### Icon Style

- Line icons, 1.5px stroke weight
- Rounded corners/caps
- Minimal detail
- Match Nuage Charcoal or Nuage Stone colors
- Consistent size: 24px default, 20px small, 32px large
- Source: Lucide Icons or similar minimalist set

### UI Elements

**Buttons:**
- Primary: Nuage Charcoal background, Mist text
- Secondary: Transparent with Charcoal border
- Border radius: 4px (subtle rounding)
- Padding: 12px 24px standard
- Hover: Subtle lift (shadow) or color shift to Blush

**Cards:**
- Background: White or Nuage Cream
- Border: None or 1px Nuage Stone (subtle)
- Border radius: 8px
- Shadow: Subtle, soft (0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.05))

**Forms:**
- Input border: 1px Nuage Stone
- Focus state: Nuage Blush border
- Border radius: 4px
- Padding: 12px 16px

### Overall Mood Words

1. **Serene** — Calm, peaceful, restful
2. **Elevated** — Premium, sophisticated, refined
3. **Soft** — Gentle, approachable, warm
4. **Modern** — Clean, contemporary, minimal
5. **Curated** — Intentional, considered, quality-focused

## Brand Application Examples

### Logo Usage
- Primary: Nuage Charcoal on Mist background
- Reversed: Mist on Charcoal (for dark sections)
- Minimum clear space: Height of the "N" on all sides
- Minimum size: 80px width for legibility

### Tone of Voice
- Calm, confident, not pushy
- French touches where natural (product names, phrases)
- Avoid slang and excessive exclamation
- Speak as a knowledgeable friend, not a salesperson

### Tagline Usage
"L'art de la detente"
- Use on hero sections, about page, packaging
- Always in italics when paired with logo
- Can be used independently as a brand sign-off
