<task type="auto">

  <files>src/components/product/StockIndicator.tsx, src/data/products.ts</files>
  <action>
Create StockIndicator component that shows inventory urgency:

**Stock levels (add to product type):**
- stockLevel: number (0-100+)
- Display logic:
  - 0: "Rupture de stock" (red, no purchase)
  - 1-5: "Plus que X en stock !" (red/orange, urgent)
  - 6-10: "Stock limité" (orange, moderate urgency)
  - 11+: "En stock" (green, no urgency display)

**Visual design:**
- Inline indicator with dot icon + text
- Color coded: red (#DC2626), orange (#F97316), green (#16A34A)
- Subtle pulse animation for urgent stock (1-5 items)
- Position: Near price on product detail, subtle on card

**Seed data enhancement:**
- Update products.ts to include stockLevel for each product
- Vary levels: 80% high stock, 15% medium, 5% low stock
- Out of stock products already use inStock=false

**Psychology:**
- Low stock creates urgency (FOMO - fear of missing out)
- Specific numbers ("Plus que 3") more effective than vague "Low stock"
- Don't overuse - feels fake if everything is "almost sold out"

**Integration:**
- Add to ProductCard (small, below rating)
- Prominent on product detail page (near CTA)
- Animate urgency indicator with pulse effect (opacity 0.7 → 1)
  </action>
